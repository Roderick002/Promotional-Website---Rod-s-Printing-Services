About Rod's Printing Services

Printing Your Ideas to Perfection Since 2019

Rod's Printing Services was founded in 2019 by Roderick Palmes, a current computer engineering student at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. Roderick's skills, passion, and past life's condition led him to create Rod's Printing Services. Rod's Printing Services humbly start on Roderick's own house offering budget-friendly products and services on an affordable price. The small business takes about 3 years before discovering a new service highlight which is the Continuous Ink Supply System (CISS) Installation which gave the business a new bright light to strive. Up until now, Rod's Printing Services continuously improving and providing customers a good quality product.

The aim of building this Promotional Website for Rod's Printing Services is to provide a platform on which we can reach out to a greater number of customers and increase our business demography. Also, to gradually introduce our brand to the public for making them aware that such quality products and services can be availed on a budget friendly prices.
